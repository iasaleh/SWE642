Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department is dedicated to advancing knowledge and research in the fields of computing, programming, artificial intelligence, software engineering, and data science. Our faculty members are highly experienced professionals who bring cutting-edge research and industry practices into the classroom.

Students in our department are equipped with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for careers in academia, technology industries, and entrepreneurship. Our programs emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation.
